Our Menu

Signature Treatments

Gel Extensions

Achieve long, durable, and natural-looking nails with our premium hard gel extensions. Perfect for weak nails, this lightweight formula is odorless and flexible. It includes cuticle care, shaping, and a gel polish color of your choice. Lasts up to 3-4 weeks with proper care.

Acrylic Sculpting

The classic choice for strength and length. We sculpt bespoke shapes to suit your fingers. Ideal for those who are hard on their hands. Our acrylics are applied with precision to prevent lifting. Choose from clear, pink, or ombre powders. Refills recommended every 2-3 weeks.

Custom Nail Art

Express yourself with hand-painted designs, 3D embellishments, chrome, and foils. From subtle minimalist lines to extravagant bridal jewels. Bring your inspiration photo or let our artists create a masterpiece unique to you. Pricing varies by complexity per finger.

Spa Pedicure

Sink into our massage chairs and treat your feet to a rejuvenating scrub and massage. Includes warm soak, callus removal, sugar scrub exfoliation, hydrating mask, and a relaxing foot massage. Finished with regular or gel polish application.

Gel Polish Overlay

Long-lasting, chip-free color applied directly to your natural nails. Perfect for those happy with their natural length but want extra strength and shine. Instant drying time under UV light. We use premium brands that protect your nail health.

Removal & Care

Safe removal of extensions or gel to preserve your natural nail integrity. Never peel your nails! Let us safely soak off old product, shape your natural nails, and apply a strengthening protein treatment and cuticle oil to restore hydration.
